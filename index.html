Our story

We looked at protein powder and said, "What if milk just did its job harder?"

Somewhere between a gym shaker, a rushed breakfast, and yet another person saying "just eat more protein," we had a thought: people already know milk. People already trust milk. People also forget to buy milk, spill protein powder, and make breakfast decisions while half-asleep.

So Provilac became the very practical, slightly overachieving answer: fresh milk with more natural protein, less lactose drama, no added sugar, and morning delivery so your fridge can act like it has its life together even when you do not.
